About

Founded in 1947, the Los Angeles Lakers are one of the most successful franchises in NBA history, with a rich legacy of legendary players and championship moments.

The team plays its home games at Crypto.com Arena in downtown Los Angeles, a venue shared with other professional sports teams and known for its vibrant atmosphere.

The Lakers' culture is defined by the “Showtime” era of fast-paced, entertaining basketball, a strong sense of teamwork, and a relentless pursuit of championships and excellence.
